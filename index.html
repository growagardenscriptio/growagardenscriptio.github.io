Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Download and install Delta Executor.
  2. Login to your Roblox app.
  3. Join the Grow a Garden game.
  4. Once in the game, open the Delta dashboard.
  5. Click on the Receive Key button.
  6. Copy the Key URL and open it in Chrome browser.
  7. Complete all verification tasks and click on Unlock Content.
  8. Copy the generated Delta Key.
  9. Return to Roblox and paste the key into the Delta Executor.
  10. Go to the official Grow a Garden Script website and copy the script.
  11. Paste the script into the Upload section of Delta.
  12. Click on Execute to run the script.
  13. Enjoy enhanced gameplay and custom features!
